Each episode is 100% original and completely unique. Each episode is 100% rare. Just like an original painting, each episode is 1-of-only-1, forever. There are no other copies. And, just as you can own a rare painting, NFTs make it possible for you to actually own each episode and to prove that ownership on the blockchain.
